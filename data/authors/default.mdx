Darwin Cloud is a physics-based cloud platform for computational chemistry, biophysics, and materials science. It provides tools for molecular modeling, simulations, and drug discovery, integrating quantum mechanics and molecular dynamics.

The platform supports tasks such as structure-based drug design, virtual screening, and predictive modeling, enabling researchers to accelerate the development of new therapeutics. It also offers solutions for materials design and optimization.

Darwin collaborates with academia and industry teams across the world to advance scientific research and innovation.
